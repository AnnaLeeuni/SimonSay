

Simon Says is a game where the computer shows the player a sequence of buttons to click, 
and the player must then repeat that sequence back. 
first, player hit the start game sign at top, 
then, there will be sequence of button being light up,
and then, player will try to remember the right sequence of the button appear
and click them in order.
each time player clicked the right button and button will be light up, 
try to slow down your clicking speed for the program to run.
If the player has clicked all the right button  in order,
The game continues, adding one button to the sequence each turn, 
and start lighting up the button from the beginning of the sequence
until the player makes a mistake
the game ends and out print "game end".
